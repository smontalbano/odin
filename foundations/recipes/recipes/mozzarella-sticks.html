Mozzarella Sticks

Mozzarella sticks are a beloved appetizer that perfectly combines gooey cheese with a crispy outer coating, creating a mouthwatering treat that's hard to resist. These golden-brown delights are made by encasing sticks of mozzarella cheese in a seasoned breadcrumb mixture and then frying or baking until perfectly crispy on the outside and deliciously melty on the inside. Whether served as a crowd-pleasing party snack or a fun finger food for family gatherings, mozzarella sticks are sure to be a hit with cheese lovers of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 12 mozzarella string cheese sticks
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup Italian breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil for frying (or cooking spray for baking)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Breading Station:

    • Set up a breading station with three shallow bowls. Place flour in the first bowl, beaten eggs in the second bowl, and breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, oregano, basil, paprika, salt, and pepper in the third bowl.

  2. Coat the Cheese Sticks:

    • Take a mozzarella stick and dredge it in the flour, shaking off any excess.
    • Dip the floured cheese stick into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.
    • Roll the cheese stick in the seasoned breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere the breadcrumbs to the cheese. Repeat this process with the remaining cheese sticks.

  3. Freeze the Mozzarella Sticks (Optional):

    • For extra crispiness, place the breaded mozzarella sticks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and freeze them for at least 30 minutes. This step helps prevent the cheese from oozing out during frying or baking.

  4. Fry or Bake the Mozzarella Sticks:

    • If frying: Heat cooking oil in a deep fryer or heavy-bottomed pot to 350°F (175°C). Carefully fry the mozzarella sticks in batches for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
    • If baking: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the breaded mozzarella sticks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Lightly spray the sticks with cooking spray. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until golden and crispy.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Serve the crispy mozzarella sticks hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as marinara, ranch, or garlic aioli.
    • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley or grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
    • Enjoy the irresistible combination of crunchy breadcrumbs and melted cheese in every bite of these homemade mozzarella sticks.
